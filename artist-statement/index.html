Artist Statement

I am a photographic artist, drawn to the documentary style of photography. Meaning that I don’t pre-visualize my images, but rather find them through the process of wandering.

Landscapes, architecture, and lost and lonely objects often inspire me. I seek to capture the essence, or soul, of my subjects, and try to steer clear of romantic clichés and show local flavour without compromising my subjects to cultural stereotypes.

My process is intuitive in nature, and my work is a reflection of self, in response to place.
